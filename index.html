TechCon 2024, was hosted by the University of Chicago Booth Technology Group in Chicago on March 22, 2024, brought together ~200 attendees with 28 expert speakers. The theme of the conference was "Embracing Disruption – Differentiation in the Age of Automated Everything" The conference aimed at developing tech careers, discussing opportunities in tech and re-inforcing the value of speed, agility and AI in tech.
